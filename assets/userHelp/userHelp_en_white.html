HUAWEI DevEco Assistant User Guide

The HUAWEI DevEco Assistant app helps you install your apps onto HUAWEI Watch GT series devices, and collects logs of how your apps run on them, so you can debug your apps and locate any faults. HUAWEI DevEco Assistant works with the Huawei Health app, so do not close Huawei Health when it is installing apps or collecting logs.


Connecting to HUAWEI Watch GT

1. In HUAWEI DevEco Assistant, touch Connect. This will open Huawei Health.

2. In Huawei Health, touch the Devices tab, then touch Add.

3. Touch Smart Watch and select the HUAWEI Watch GT model you want to connect to.

4. Touch PAIR and follow the onscreen instructions to connect the watch to your phone.


Installing an App

1. Copy the .hap file of the app to the /sdcard/haps/ directory on the phone.

2. In HUAWEI DevEco Assistant, touch Apps.

3. Select the target .hap file, then touch Install. Once the installation is complete, a success message will appear.


Obtaining Logs

Touch Logs to start collecting logs of apps.


Log collection rules:

•The HUAWEI Watch GT sends logs to HUAWEI DevEco Assistant 1 KB at a time. This means if the log size is 2.5 KB, the HUAWEI Watch GT will have to send the logs in three parts. Once log collection starts, you can use the HUAWEI Watch GT and view the logs created in HUAWEI DevEco Assistant.

•If the collected log is smaller than 1 KB, touch the Stop button so that the logs can be displayed in HUAWEI DevEco Assistant.

•A maximum of 10,000 logs can be displayed in HUAWEI DevEco Assistant. If the number of logs exceeds 10,000, HUAWEI DevEco Assistant will automatically delete the earliest 2000 logs.
